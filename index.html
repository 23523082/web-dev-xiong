About Us

We are a team of students from Universitas Islam Indonesia formed through the Web Programming course. This group brings together individuals with different perspectives and skills, working collaboratively to design and develop this website. Our teamwork reflects not only our technical learning, but also our ability to communicate, collaborate, and grow throughout the course.

2

About This Page

This website is the final project for the Web Programming course. The homepage represents our group identity, while each personal page introduces individual members in more detail. Through this project, we apply the concepts and skills learned in class, combining design, functionality, and creativity into a complete web experience.

3

Our Journey: UII to NXU

Our academic journey began at Universitas Islam Indonesia (UII) in Yogyakarta. As part of this course, we had the opportunity to engage in an international academic experience at Nanjing Xiaozhuang University (NXU) in China. This journey enriched our learning by exposing us to a new academic environment and cross-cultural perspectives.
