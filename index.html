Our Journey

The story of WEB-DEV-XIONG's evolution

2021

The Beginning

WEB-DEV-XIONG started as a small study group of passionate web developers. Our initial focus was on mastering HTML and CSS fundamentals.

2022

Team Expansion

We expanded our team and began working on collaborative projects. Added JavaScript to our skill stack and started building interactive websites.

2023

Framework Mastery

Started learning modern frameworks like React and Vue.js. Began contributing to open-source projects and participated in hackathons.

2024

Full-Stack Development

Expanded into back-end development with Node.js and databases. Started building full-stack applications and launched our first major project.

Present

Web Development Platform

Now we're building a comprehensive platform to help others learn web development through practical projects and collaborative learning.
